Espresso House

You can find plenty of these cafes around the city. Stockholm dwellers like going to Espresso House lo hang out and meet their friends. There you can try some sweets, drink a cup of coffee or even eat a large meatburger. Prices are not very high according to locals.

×

Espresso House is the largest coffeehouse chain in the Nordics.Founded in Lund, Sweden in 1996 by Elisabet and Charles Asker, it now has over 255 locations throughout Sweden, Norway, Finland and Denmark.
Opening Hours: Monday-Sunday

Alskade Traditioner

The most famous cafe with traditional scandinavian food. Best for lunches, dinners and Instagram photos. Interior nostalgic for the past century, the time of Elvis Presley. Inside there is a real automatic plate, many old books and magazines.
