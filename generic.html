About our project

The aim is to make your trip wonderful!

Thank you for visiting our project page. We worked very hard for weeks to make it interesting and beautiful. Here we have collected local`s opinions about the city, the coolest places, the most trendy cafes and just places where you can have a good time. If you are planning a trip to Stockholm, we advise you to study the content carefully so then you will be completely ready for the journey!

You are definitely not mistaken with the choice of the city, because in Stockholm there are benevolent people who will help you. By the way, the population of Stockholm is less than a million. The climate in the city is very pleasant, because it is not hot in summer, and in winter you do not feel bad frost. You can move to Stockholm by metro and trams. It is worth noting that in this city there are three airports!

Medicine and science will thrive in Stockholm, so many people come there to be treated and studied. Stockholm is known for its rich culture. Famous people lived and worked here, various cultural institutions were founded. In 1998 he was elected European cultural capital. Also the city is famous for its theaters and festivals. More information you will learn in our articles. Enjoy travelling.
